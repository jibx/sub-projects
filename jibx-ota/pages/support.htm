Commercial Support and Training

Commercial support and training for both JiBX and JiBX/OTA is provided through Sosnoski Software Solutions, Inc. (for clients in the United States) and Sosnoski Software Associates Ltd (for clients worldwide). Email JiBX lead developer Dennis Sosnoski to find out about rates and scheduling for all JiBX/OTA-related support and training.

Free Support

Free support is available for JiBX/OTA through the JiBX email lists. Both JiBX developers and other JiBX and JiBX/OTA users will often respond to emails on the lists, though there is no guarantee of a response to any particular issue.
